444What is Alabaster anyway?

mahditer Alabaster was originally a theme for the popular documentation tool Sphinx. This theme port to Hugo theme is all about documentations. It should allow you to easily create a documentation for your projects.

Fabric and Invoke are two examples of the Alabaster theme in the wild.

1The following pages will guide you through the setup, introduce you to the very basics of Hugo and show you the capabilities of this theme. Have a look at the Roadmap if you’re curious about what comes next.
